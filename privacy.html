Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 24, 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how Bulk Fuel Mobile (“we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, shares, and protects information related to the use of our mobile application, Bulk Fuel Mobile, designed for bulk fuel tracking, management, and analytics.

By using Bulk Fuel Mobile, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to provide, improve, and personalize our services:

a. Personal Information
- Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, company name, and other relevant details to create and manage your account.
- Contact Information: This may include phone numbers and email addresses, which are used to communicate updates or technical support.

b. Usage Information
- Device Information: We may collect details about the device used to access the app, including model, operating system, and device identifiers.
- Log Data: Information like IP address, access times, and interaction data may be collected to help us monitor and improve the app.

c. Transactional Information
If you make purchases within the app, we may collect billing and payment information to facilitate transactions. Payment data is processed by third-party payment providers and is not stored by us directly.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes, including:
- Service Delivery: To operate and maintain the app, fulfill requests, and provide our core services.
- Customer Support: To assist with troubleshooting, answer inquiries, and improve user satisfaction.
- Improvement and Analytics: To analyze usage patterns, improve app functionality, and enhance our offerings.
- Communication: To send service updates, account-related notifications, and marketing communications (with your consent).
- Compliance: To comply with legal requirements and enforce our Terms of Service.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your information. However, we may share it in the following ways:
- With Service Providers: We may share data with third-party vendors, consultants, and other service providers who perform tasks on our behalf, such as payment processing, customer support, or analytics.
- Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to respond to legal processes.
- Business Transfers: If we undergo a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the successor entity.

4. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption, secure networks, and access controls. However, no data transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. You acknowledge that there are inherent risks in data transfer, and we cannot completely ensure data security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. You can request the deletion of your personal information by contacting us at brock@brockai.com.

6. Your Choices

- Access and Update: You may access, update, or correct your personal information via your account settings.
- Marketing Opt-Out: You can unsubscribe from marketing emails by following the unsubscribe instructions included in our communications.
- Location Data: You can control location data collection by adjusting your mobile device settings.
- Do Not Track Signals: Our app does not respond to “Do Not Track” signals.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you are aware of any information from a minor being shared with us, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our app and updating the “Effective Date.” Continued use of the app after changes implies acceptance of the updated policy.
