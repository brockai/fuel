Bulk Fuel Mobile was developed under the leadership of the Birch Mountain management team in close collaboration with experienced field operators and administrators. Iterative Software Development

An iterative development cycle is a software development approach that breaks down the project into small, manageable segments called iterations. Each iteration goes through the entire software development lifecycle (SDLC) — planning, designing, coding, testing, and deploying — but for a specific subset of features or functionality. This process is repeated in multiple cycles, gradually enhancing the software until the full product is developed.

  • Planning: The team identifies the objectives, requirements, and features to focus on, the scope of each iteration is kept small and manageable.
  • Design: The team agree on a user interface design and associated behaviours based on what was learned from previous iterations, allowing for flexibility and improvements.
  • Development: Developers code the features and fucntionality that were planned.
  • Testing: Once development of a feature or is complete, the new functionality is tested by our devlopement team and then made available for user acceptance testing by your team.
  • Review and Feedback: After testing, the team reviews the results using a working version of the software to gather feedback from stakeholders or users. This feedback is critical and shapes the priorities for the next iteration.
  • Deployment: At the end of the iteration, the developed features are automatically deployed to your production environment to be used. This frequent deployment of working software allows the product to evolve and grow incrementally.
  • Repeat: After one iteration ends, the process repeats. The team begins the next iteration, incorporating feedback and improving or expanding on the previously developed features.

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my account free of charge?

Yes, your account can be cancelled at any time free of charge.

Can Bulk Fuel Mobile run on my server or hosting provider?

Yes, Bulk Fuel Mobile can run anywhere. Please contact us to learn more.

Are there any licensing fees associated with Bulk Fuel Mobile?

No, Bulk Fuel Mobile is entirely built with OpenSource software.

Do I need Geotab to run real-time data integration?

No, however Geotab is used to automatically record load and offload times so that the operator does not have to manually enter those times. Shifts and Delivery Board are always real-time.

Can Bulk Fuel Mobile be customized?

Yes, in fact the system was designed in a modular way so that other components and service integrations can be implemented. Please contact us to learn more.
